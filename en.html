About Us

Kostadinovi 2020 is a family-owned company based in Blagoevgrad, Bulgaria, specializing in precision metalworking. We combine modern equipment with skilled craftsmanship to deliver reliable results.

Our Services

  • Turning – precision machining of cylindrical parts
  • Milling – production of complex shapes and surfaces
  • Welding – MIG, TIG, and electrode welding of various metals
  • Custom fabrication – based on technical drawings
  • Small and medium series – tailored production volumes
